Heres how it works:

  1. Gather your team

    2. You can play with the people you live with if you like but if you want to connect with your family around the world, that is fine too! We recommend no more than six people per team though, otherwise you get a lot of people shouting over each other and no one feels like they've been heard. Also less people means its easier to share the loot.

  2. Decide on a team leader

    3. Only one of you needs to fill out the answers online so it is best to decide who it is who will be writing that. It also means that they can have final say when there are various people arguing over answers. We recommend picking someone who has good spelling skills and good diplomancy skills.

  3. Connect with your team

    4. There are various different ways to connect with your team. Below are a few options that you can explore though often, WhatsApp is the simplest, or Zoom if you want to look at everyone's faces.

  4. Head to the Live page at the appointed time

    5. Check our schedule to make sure what time the quiz is on. Our times are in GMT so if playing with people in another country, be sure to adjust accordingly. Click on the button below and the quiz will automatically start streaming at the appointed time!
